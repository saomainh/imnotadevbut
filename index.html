I'm not a dev,
but

...but here I am,

trying my best. Well, to be honest, that’s not accurate. I did took IT class in high school, it was named NSI for Numérique et Sciences de l’Informatique. That was two years ago, and it probably remains my best memory from school,

Why Holberton?

To keep it simple, my whole life, I’ve been surrounded by developers : my dad ; my online friends ; my ex lovers ; my new friends... And I, little by little, started to be interested in IT myself. At first, I was going to study art, but my plans changed and I decided to do code too. One small Issue : I don’t have the bac. That’s when I heard of Holberton.

What's this website?

Nothing more than a classic portfolio. I said that I’m not a dev (yet), but I still am a lot of other things : I draw, I write, and I take photos. Originally, I made a mockup on Figma, but it seems I can't do it following the level 2's instructions. Hover your mouse over the picture :)

Have fun visiting "I'm not a dev, but"!
